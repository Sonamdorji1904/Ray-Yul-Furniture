Ray-Yul Furniture: Empowering Communities. Redefining Craftsmanship.

Ray-Yul Furniture is a forward-thinking Bhutanese enterprise committed to delivering durable, high-quality metal and wooden furniture that reflects both modern functionality and timeless craftsmanship. Founded in 2022 and headquartered in Hurungpam, Mongar, Ray-Yul Furniture is more than a manufacturing business — it is a mission-driven enterprise that blends entrepreneurship, skill development, and community empowerment.

Our Origin Story

The foundation of Ray-Yul Furniture is rooted in resilience and transformation. Sangay Chophel, our visionary founder, established the company after overcoming significant personal and academic challenges. A graduate of Nangkor Central School, Sangay redirected his life journey through technical training in welding at Jigme Wangchuk Power Training Institute, and furniture-making courses at Rangjung.

He further equipped himself with managerial expertise through entrepreneurship programs at Rangjung Technical Training Institute and the ITEC program in India, setting the stage for a business built on excellence and purpose.

What We Do

  • Customized wooden and metal furniture
  • Residential and office furnishings
  • Durable, long-lasting products with a warranty of service
  • Free repair and support for furniture purchased from us

Our enterprise is defined by innovation, craftsmanship, and service excellence,
ensuring that every product meets the needs of modern customers while maintaining

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through job creation, skill development, and the delivery of affordable, premium-quality furniture that enhances both lifestyle and livelihoods.

Our Vision

To become Bhutan’s leading socially-responsible furniture enterprise, recognized for craftsmanship, customer satisfaction, and its contributions to community and economic development.

Our Achievements

Despite the challenges of launching an enterprise in a competitive market, Ray-Yul Furniture has:

  • Secured support from the DHI BIZAP Fund
  • Established a growing customer base across eastern Bhutan
  • Created employment opportunities for local youth
  • Earned trust through transparent service and lasting products
